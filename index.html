The Claremont Greenshirts (AKA the Greens, Greenies, etc.) are a fun-loving gender expansive club Ultimate Frisbee team at the Claremont Colleges in Southern California!

  The team practices year-round and competes against other collegiate D-III teams, with our primary competitive season being in the spring.

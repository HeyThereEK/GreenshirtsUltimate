Practice

  We practice 3 times a week! Most semesters our practice schedule is Monday, Wednesday, Sunday 5:00-7:00pm, though this varies some semesters based on availability of field space. Follow our instagram to stay updated on the schedule for this upcoming Fall 2023 semester!

  Greens get to determine their own level of commitment. This means that lots of Greens come to every practice (it’s just so fun and awesome) but others can only make it once or twice a week (or just every now and then!) and that’s totally okay! We recognize that everyone has other demands in life, so feel comfortable only committing what you have capacity for!
